Knowing about the Laws of Persuasion for Your Insurance Leads

Here are several laws of persuasion that have been researched and written by reputable psychologist-Robert Cialdini.

- Reciprocation Law; It emphasizes you to give so you will get something in return. To offer your insurance product, you need to approach people by giving them small gifts. In return, they will buy your product or service because they feel indebt to you.

- Contrast Law; It's more on comparing price with others. If you offer someone cheaper life insurance program than what other have, you will win. Nobody rejects benefit including lower price. Therefore, it can be the best way to attract people.

- Scarcity Law; People always tend to be more interested with unusual thing that different from others. Thus, you need to offer rare or new products.

- Authority Law; It is often that people will follow trusted person's recommendation. It will create trust that what you offer is reliable and recommended. You may use insurance expert's recommendation in selling insurance.
